About Mary


Program Officer at the African Leaders Malaria Alliance. Full-time Minnesota enthusiast.

I'm a native Minnesotan, currently transplanted to NY (though I'd like to try out CA for a change).

I work for the African Leaders Malaria Alliance, a nonprofit focused on working with African Heads of State and Governent to end malaria-related deaths by 2015.

I'm passionate about spending time with family, friends and colleagues.

On most weekends, you can expect me to be catching up on my Hulu queue, reading overdue books from the New York Public Library, and trying out new recipes from The Kitchn.
